# 📄 IEEE ICCSP 2025 — Publication Reference

## Conference

**IEEE International Conference on Communication and Signal Processing (ICCSP) 2025**

## Paper

**Title:** Smart Agri-Bot — AI-Integrated IoT Approach for Autonomous Agricultural Systems

**Authors:** Maithili Sadashiv Joshi et al.

**Status:** Peer-reviewed, published at IEEE ICCSP 2025

## Abstract Summary

This paper documents the design, implementation, and experimental results of the Smart Agri-Bot — an IoT-enabled autonomous agricultural robot that integrates embedded sensors, microcontrollers, and AI decision-making algorithms for precision seed sowing and smart irrigation. The system was validated across multiple real-world field test cycles.

## What the Paper Covers

- Full system architecture (hardware + software)
- AI decision algorithm design and threshold tuning
- Sensor integration methodology
- Field test results and performance evaluation
- Comparison with traditional manual irrigation methods

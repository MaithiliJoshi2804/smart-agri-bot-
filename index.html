How It Works

Four tightly integrated stages — from raw sensor input to physical autonomous action in the field.

01
📡

Sensor Data Collection

Embedded sensors (soil moisture, temperature, humidity) continuously stream live field data to the microcontroller.

02
🧠

AI Decision Engine

Custom AI algorithms analyze real-time sensor readings and dynamically decide when and how much to irrigate, and optimal seed depth.

03
🤖

Autonomous Actuation

Motor drivers and actuators execute irrigation commands and precision seed sowing without any manual intervention.

04
🔄

Feedback & Iteration

Post-action sensor readings feed back into the system, enabling the bot to self-correct and improve across multiple field cycles.
